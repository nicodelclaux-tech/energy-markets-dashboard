# Charting Analytics

## When to Use

- New or revised charts.
- KPI strips, comparative analytics, and ranking displays.
- Converting processed series into readable visual structures.

## Procedure

1. Choose a chart form that matches the analytical question.
2. Keep historical, forward, and forecast views separate and labeled.
3. Prefer precomputed analytics when the browser would otherwise own complex logic.
4. Make missing or partial coverage visible in the chart treatment.

## Output

- Chart requirements.
- Data-shaping needs.
- Readability and integrity checks.
